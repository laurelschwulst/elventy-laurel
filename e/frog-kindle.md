Inspired by the <a href="https://www.bopsecrets.org/gateway/passages/basho-frog.htm">famous frog haiku</a> by Bashô, I wanted to create my own translation.

古池や<br>
蛙飛び込む<br>
水の音<br>

ふるいけや<br>
かわずとびこむ<br>
みずのおと

<i>Furu ike ya<br>
kawazu tobikomu<br>
mizu no oto</i>

<b>Old pond!<br>
frog jumps in<br>
water’s sound</b>
